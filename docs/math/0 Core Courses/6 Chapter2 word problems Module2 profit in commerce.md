## 8.Rate of Change Formulas
::: wrap  
1. $a_{\textsf{before}} \xrightarrow{\textsf{increased by }p\%} a_{\textsf{after}}=a_{\textsf{before}}(1+p\%)$  
   $a_{\textsf{before}}=\frac{a_{\textsf{after}}}{1+p\%}$
2. $a_{\textsf{before}} \xrightarrow{\textsf{decreased by }p\%} a_{\textsf{after}}=a_{\textsf{before}}(1-p\%)$  
   $a_{\textsf{before}}=\frac{a_{\textsf{after}}}{1-p\%}$
3. $A\textsf{ is }p\%\textsf{ greater than }B \Longleftrightarrow \frac{A-B}{B}=p\% \Longleftrightarrow A=B(1+p\%)$
4. $A\textsf{ is }p\%\textsf{ less than }B \Longleftrightarrow \frac{B-A}{B}=p\% \Longleftrightarrow A=B(1-p\%)$
5. $A\textsf{ is }p\%\textsf{ of }B \Longleftrightarrow A=B\cdot p\%$
- Note: $A\textsf{ is }p\%\textsf{ greater than }B \ne B\textsf{ is }p\%\textsf{ less than }A$
:::

### $\fbox{11}\:$The planned sales for the store this month are $\small 20\textsf{K USD}$. Because of a promotional event, $\small 60\%$ of the plan has been completed in the first half of the month. If the store needs to exceed the plan by $\small 25\%$ by the end of the month, what amount of sales must be completed in the second half of the month $\small \mathpunct{?}$.
::: wrap  
$
\begin{array}{lllll}
\textsf{(A)} \: 12\textsf{K USD} &
\textsf{(B)} \: 13\textsf{K USD} &
\textsf{(C)} \: 14\textsf{K USD} & \\
\textsf{(D)} \: 15\textsf{K USD} &
\textsf{(E)} \: 16\textsf{K USD} & \\
\end{array}
$  
:::
#### Solution
::: wrap  
$
\begin{array}{ll}
\textsf{sales}_{2\textsf{nd half}}=20(1+25\%)-20\cdot60\% \\
20\cdot1.25-20\cdot0.6 \\
25-12=13 \\
\end{array}
$  
:::  
#### Conclusion
- Derived Solution  
  ::: wrap
  > $\boldsymbol{(B)}$  
  > According to the Solution, get $\textsf{sales}_{2\textsf{nd half}}=13$, so choose $B$. 

  :::
