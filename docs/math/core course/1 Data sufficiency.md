## 1.Definition
::: wrap  
$
\begin{array}{lllll}
\textsf{(1)} \: \textsf{condition one} &
\textsf{(2)} \: \textsf{condition two} & \\
\textsf{(A)} \: \textsf{(1)} &
\textsf{(B)} \: \textsf{(2)} & \\
\textsf{(C)} \: \textsf{(1)}+\textsf{(2)} &  
\textsf{(D)} \: \textsf{(1)},\textsf{(2)} & \\
\textsf{(E)} \: \varnothing & \\
\end{array}
$  
:::  
- __(A)__: condition one alone is __sufficient__, but condition two alone is __not sufficient__.
- __(B)__: condition two alone is __sufficient__, but condition one alone is __not sufficient__.
- __(C)__: __neither condition one nor two alone is sufficient__, but __the two conditions together are sufficient__.
- __(D)__: __each condition alone is sufficient__.
- __(E)__: __neither condition one nor two alone is sufficient__, and __even together they are not sufficient__.

### $\fbox{15}\:\small \frac{1}{x}\gt x$
::: wrap  
$
\begin{array}{lllll}
\textsf{(1)} \: x\lt-1 &
\textsf{(2)} \: 0\lt x\lt 1 & \\
\textsf{(A)} \: \textsf{(1)} &
\textsf{(B)} \: \textsf{(2)} & \\
\textsf{(C)} \: \textsf{(1)}+\textsf{(2)} &  
\textsf{(D)} \: \textsf{(1)},\textsf{(2)} & \\
\textsf{(E)} \: \varnothing & \\
\end{array}
$  
:::
#### Solution
- $\textcolor{#228B22}{\fbox{1}}\:$Solve by separating the sign of $x$  
  ::: wrap  
  $
  \begin{array}{ll}
  \textcolor{#228B22}{\scriptsize\fbox{1}}\:\textsf{let }x>0 \\
  1>x^2 \Longrightarrow -1\lt x\lt1 \\
  \left\{ x\,|\, x\lt-1 \right\} \not\subset \left\{ x\,|\, -1\lt x\lt1 \right\} & \textcolor{#9333EA}{\textsf{verify condition }\textsf{(1)}} \\
  \left\{ x\,|\, 0\lt x\lt 1 \right\} \subset \left\{ x\,|\, -1\lt x\lt1 \right\} & \textcolor{#9333EA}{\textsf{verify condition }\textsf{(2)}} \\
  \textcolor{#228B22}{\scriptsize\fbox{2}}\:\textsf{let }x<0 \\
  1\lt x^2 \Longrightarrow x\gt1 \lor x\lt-1 \\
  \left\{ x\,|\, x\lt-1 \right\} \subset \left\{ x\,|\, x\lt-1 \right\} & \textcolor{#9333EA}{\textsf{verify condition }\textsf{(1)}} \\
  \left\{ x\,|\, 0\lt x\lt 1 \right\} \not\subset \left\{ x\,|\, x\gt1 \lor x\lt-1 \right\}  & \textcolor{#9333EA}{\textsf{verify condition }\textsf{(2)}} \\
  \end{array}
  $  
  :::
- $\textcolor{#228B22}{\fbox{2}}\:$Solve by using the sign chart method  
  ::: wrap  
  $
  \begin{array}{ll}
  x-\frac{1}{x}\lt0 \\
  \frac{x^2}{x}-\frac{1}{x}=\frac{x^2-1}{x} \\
  \frac{(x-1)(x+1)}{x}\lt0 \\
  (x-1)(x+1)=0 \rightarrow x=1\lor-1 & \textsf{numerator}=0 \\
  x=0 & \textsf{denominator}=0 \\
  x=1,-1,0 & \textsf{critical points} \\
  (-\infty, -1), (-1,0), (0,1), (1, \infty) & \textsf{intervals} \\
  (-\infty, -1)\textsf{ analysis} \\
  x-1=- \\
  x+1=- \\
  x=- \\
  (–)(–)(–)=– \\
  (-1,0)\textsf{ analysis} \\
  x-1=- \\
  x+1=+ \\
  x=- \\
  (–)(+)(–)=+ \\
  (0,1)\textsf{ analysis} \\
  x-1=- \\
  x+1=+ \\
  x=+ \\
  (–)(+)(+)=– \\
  (1, \infty)\textsf{ analysis} \\
  x-1=+ \\
  x+1=+ \\
  x=+ \\
  (+)(+)(+)=+ \\
  \textcolor{#9333EA}{\textsf{verify condition }\textsf{(1)}} \\
  \left\{ x\,|\, x\lt-1 \right\} \subset (-\infty, -1) \\
  \textcolor{#9333EA}{\textsf{verify condition }\textsf{(2)}} \\
  \left\{ x\,|\, 0\lt x\lt 1 \right\} \subset (0,1) \\
  \end{array}
  $  
  :::  
  ![Question data sufficiency figure Q-15 solve-1.svg](../../public/math/core%20course/Question%20data%20sufficiency%20figure%20Q-15%20solve-1.svg)  
#### Conclusion
- Derived Solution  
  ::: wrap
  > $\boldsymbol{(D)}$  
  > According to the Solution, get $\textsf{(1)}\,✅\;,\textsf{(2)}\,✅\;$, so choose $D$.
